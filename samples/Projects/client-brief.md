# Client Brief: Kairos Health

## Overview

Kairos Health is a wellness startup building a mobile app for guided breathwork and meditation. They need a brand identity and landing page designed before their seed round closes in April.

## Deliverables

1. **Wordmark logo** — clean, modern, approachable
2. **Color palette** — calming but not clinical
3. **Landing page design** — single scroll, email capture, app preview
4. **Social templates** — Instagram and LinkedIn formats

## Brand Direction

- *Calm confidence* — not whisper-quiet, not loud
- Feels like a deep breath, not a hospital waiting room
- References: Headspace (approachability), Oura (premium feel), Linear (clarity)
